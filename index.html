Text to Music Generation

What Our Project Does

In our project, Parameter-based Music Generation, we harness the power of Artificial Intelligence to revolutionize music creation. Through advanced techniques like Natural Language Processing and Generative AI, we aim to empower users to generate personalized music compositions effortlessly. By bridging the gap between user input and AI-generated music, we democratize music production, offering limitless creative possibilities for individuals of all musical backgrounds.

Problem

Generating quality music quickly presents challenges for beginners as well as the experts. From mastering melodies and rhythms to perfecting harmonies, the process demands time and expertise. Bridging this gap between creativity and ease of use is a universal goal in music composition, inspiring innovation in tools and techniques. Whether someone who does not even know music or experts, musicians seek efficient methods to unleash their creativity while meeting deadlines and artistic expectations.
